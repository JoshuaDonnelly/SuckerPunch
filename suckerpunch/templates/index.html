So, What is Sucker Punch?

Sucker Punch is an assistant application for those who want to practice their boxing skills in a game like format. In other words, we make shadow boxing at home fun and engaging, helping you keep fit or sharpen your uppercut!

What do I need to do?

Using our integrated hardware, you simply position the device 5-15 meters from yourself with the dome of the Motion Sensor facing you. Then launch one of our sessions and begin shadow boxing. The site will give you feedback as you go along. Give it a try!
